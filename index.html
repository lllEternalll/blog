ROG Strix SCAR 18 G835 (2025) Gaming Beast

Overview

The ASUS ROG Strix SCAR 18 G835 (2025) is a true powerhouse designed for elite gamers and creators. With the cutting-edge Intel Core Ultra 9 275HX processor and the next-gen NVIDIA GeForce RTX 5090 GPU, it delivers unmatched performance in AAA games and demanding workloads. The 18-inch Nebula HDR display offers stunning visuals with ultra-smooth refresh rates, while the refined cyberpunk-inspired design reflects the ROG spirit—bold, futuristic, and uncompromising.

Comparison with G834 (2024)

Compared to its predecessor, the G834 (2024), the G835 brings significant performance upgrades. The G834 was already a beast with its Intel Core i9 14900HX and RTX 4090, but the G835 takes it further with the new Intel Core Ultra 9 275HX and the latest RTX 5090. In addition to raw performance, the cooling system has also been improved to maintain optimal temperatures under load. These enhancements make the G835 an even more future-ready choice for high-end gaming and professional tasks.
